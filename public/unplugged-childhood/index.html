What we
celebrate.

Thousands of publishers, educators, creators, and authors are doing extraordinary work — helping children explore, create, move, read, play, and connect. Unplugged Childhood exists to find them, recognize them, and make it easy for families to discover them.

01

Find the people doing the work

We spent a year researching over 1,500 publishers, authors, educators, and creators who are actively promoting active, curious, and engaged childhoods. This is the network that emerged.

02

Give them the recognition they deserve

A Founding Member profile is a public celebration — a statement that this publisher, creator, or author has done something genuinely valuable for children and the families raising them.

03

Help families find what's good

Parents shouldn't have to dig. Every member profile is written to help families understand who this person is, what they stand for, and where to find their best work.
