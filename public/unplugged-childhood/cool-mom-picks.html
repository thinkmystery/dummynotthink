Why selected

Cool Mom Picks has been a trusted voice in family lifestyle publishing since 2006, building an editorial footprint spanning kids' activities, toys, family travel, gift guides, and modern parenting — always with a practical, parent-first perspective.

The publication has consistently published screen-free family content, including practical guides to entertaining children without relying on devices. That alignment — balanced, realistic, and helpful — is precisely what the Unplugged Childhood network is looking for.
