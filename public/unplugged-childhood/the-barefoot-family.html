Why selected

The Barefoot Family has built one of the most authentic and engaged family audiences on social media — not by advocating for anything, but simply by showing what an active, unhurried childhood looks like when a family commits to it fully.

Their content — outdoor mornings, handmade toys, unstructured afternoons in the woods — consistently generates the kind of warmth and engagement that polished parenting accounts rarely achieve. That authenticity is exactly what Unplugged Childhood exists to celebrate and amplify.
